What is Skill Level?

Your skill level serves as an indicator of your progress and experience as a transcriber on the WorkHub. As you complete transcription jobs, QA sessions, or reviews on the TranscribeMe WorkHub, you move up the ranks and your skill level increases. Every audio minute you successfully complete and submit counts toward your skill level progression. You can check your current level and amount of audio minutes you've processed in the header of the WorkHub at any time. You can also see there the number of minutes required to move up to the next level.

After a certain number of levels, you receive a new badge (such as Capitalization Hero or Champion), like a rank title. You can check out how many more minutes you need to complete in order to receive the next badge in the WorkHub header, the same place as where you check how many minutes are required for the next level.

Your skill level and badge don’t affect your pay rates or access to work; it just lets you know how you're doing. Keep up the good work!
